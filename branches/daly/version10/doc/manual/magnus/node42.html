User class Word



What and Where

The class class Word : public Elt
is defined in magnus/back_end/Elt/include/Word.h.

A Word may be used outside the context of any particular group, so the standard operations on words are provided. However, several subclasses of Group use Word to represent their elements, so they also have methods for, e.g., multiplying Words, which may also, e.g., freely reduce.
